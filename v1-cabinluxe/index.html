About European Hut

A European Escape, Minutes from Cairo

The European Hut (الكوخ الأوروبي) brings the charm of European countryside living to Egypt. Located just half an hour past Sheikh Zayed on the Cairo-Alexandria Desert Road, our exclusive retreat offers four beautifully designed cabins — each with its own heated pool and complete privacy.

Whether you're planning a romantic getaway, a family retreat, or a women-only gathering, our huts provide the perfect blend of modern luxury and serene escape from the city.
